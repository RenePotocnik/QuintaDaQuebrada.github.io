Very worm and dry on summer, gentle temperature and very few rainy days on winter. The unit offers you 8 Bungalows all with a very beautiful view over the Atlantic Ocean, TV and Telephone. Nature

The surrounding areas are boundless! There is a long sidewalk connecting to all the tiny little houses (casinhas), bordered by gardens filled with aromatic herbs, carnations and roses, along with hedges of laurel, dill and other rare native plants from Madeira's forests. In the nearby areas, plantations of papaya tress, sugar cane, figue and banana trees and vineyards, offer the Comples a feeling of peace and harmony with nature. To complete your morning, nothing better than a dive into the farm's swimming pool! We also want to enhance the fact that there is an enclosed parking lot for the security of your car.

The place

Enjoy the pleasant weather at Quinta da Quebrada, where the sun shines on our side of the island almost every day. The summers are warm and dry. You will relax in the comfortable climate and solitude of our complex. Enjoy the beautiful view of the Atlantic Ocean from our private cottage.
